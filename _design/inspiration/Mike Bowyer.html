About Us

Mike is an experienced entrepreneur, business development and services manager, as well as a virtual assistant team leader.  Over the years, he has helped many small and medium sized businesses to not only achieve their objectives, goals and aspirations, but in many cases to exceed them.

Mike started his early career in the banking and finance industry, moving into more technical and business development roles as his career progressed. Studies were an important part of his early career, where he gained a BEC National Diploma and Masters in Business Administration.

This background of experience and academic qualifications, enabled Mike to move into senior positions with Major Blue Chip organisations in the IT industry, including Microsoft Corp, where he held Channel Management roles in the Business Solutions Unit branded Microsoft Dynamics.

Having worked alongside business owners and directors for many years now, he understands what’s required to manage a business within tight deadlines and increasing workloads, and knows how to find the right skills at the appropriate time, to fulfil project requirements and milestones effectively.

Mike and his team specialise in a wide range of online business services, including , technical website and software development, consulting and general business administration solutions. These services and activities are offered with complete confidence to all clients, and prospective clients, who have aspirations to optimise the performance of their business and enterprise.

We are here to help you “Optimise Your Business Performance.”

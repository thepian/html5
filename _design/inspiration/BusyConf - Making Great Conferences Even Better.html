Organizing a conference?

Collect proposals. Choose the best.

BusyConf gives speakers a place to submit proposals for your conference, then lets you privately rate proposals, helping you (and your team) select the best ones.

Keep up-to-date speaker info.

Speakers can keep their bios, avatars, and other info up to date in BusyConf, so you don't have to.

Build the schedule.

Add tracks, tweak time slots, rearrange talks, and make last minute changes without an ounce of coding.

Publish the schedule.

BusyConf hosts the online schedule for your conference forever. You can even embed it on your own website.

Give them something to remember you by.

Attendees with smart phones get a beautiful app icon with your conference logo when they add the schedule to their home screen.

Attending a conference?

Have it all. Fast.

BusyConf gives you a fast and beautiful interface to navigate the entire conference. Check out the speakers, gauge the talks, and make your choice.

Is that a schedule in your pocket?

BusyConf looks great in your browser, but it doesn't stop there. Optimized versions for your tablet and your smart phone keep you clued in while you're on the go.

No Wi-Fi? No problem.

Conferences are notorious for having slow, fickle, or even no Wi-Fi. But thanks to HTML5 application caching, you'll always be able to see the schedule. Just download it before hand.

Got an iPhone?

Pull up the schedule in Safari and then add it to your home screen. Each conference gets its own glorious app icon.

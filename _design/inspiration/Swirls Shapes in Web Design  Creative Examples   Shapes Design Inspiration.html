Swirls Shapes in Web Design: Creative Examples

By Oksana Khristenko on 2011/03/22

Category: Shapes Design Inspiration

Swirls shapes give a creative and elegant look to a website interface. View 12 swirls shapes examples to get web design inspiration.

Tags: Abstract and Creative;

Elegant Swirls Shapes in Web Design

Here are 12 beautiful swirls inspired web designs. All websites but one in this showcase use brown as main or secondary color. Pure coincidence or more that that?

Irvineacosta.com

Bold contrast swirls on the background – a really nice combination of warm colours, swirls shapes and splatters.

Chamadigital.com

This website design uses pretty swirls to decorate the main banner image – a great combination of golden swirls shapes and wood background.

Blogsolid.com

This design uses elegant swirls shapes to decorate the main banner image.

Richbdesign.com

Orange swirls and brown background come very well together, what do you think?

Beanstalkapp.com

Seamless swirls over the background and well-thought-out white space give the design a charming and inspiring look & feel.

Creativestate.com

Light elegant swirls silhouettes used on a slightly darker background – a beautiful combination of beige and pale greyish greens.

Cotr.tv

Example of lovely swirl ornaments used together with paper textures and wood backgrounds.

20onepixels.com

Dark prickly swirls on a light bckground – a cute combination of reds, blacks, greys, and browns.

Lbdsociety.com

This website design uses large smooth semi-transparent swirls on the main banner’s background.

Imnotplaying.com

Example of nice twisted swirls shapes in three colours – blue, chocolate and white.

Ambercouch.co.uk

Example of swirls shapes on the header – a beautiul combination of light blue, white and dark greyish blue colors.

Rhaya.com

Example of light blue, even almost turquoise swirls over the light blue background.
